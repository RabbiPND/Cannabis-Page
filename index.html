About Our Company

Weedies Restaurant

Welcome to Weedies, where we blend relaxation, community, and the finest cannabis experiences into one remarkable destination. Nestled in the heart of Johannesburg, our cannabis cafe is a haven for both seasoned enthusiasts and curious newcomers

At Weedies, we strive to create an inviting and comfortable atmosphere where patrons can savor high-quality cannabis products, savor delectable treats, and foster connections with fellow enthusiasts. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are here to guide you through our diverse menu, ensuring you find the perfect strain, edible, or infused beverage to suit your preferences.

Whether you're seeking a tranquil space to unwind, engage in stimulating conversations, or simply explore the world of cannabis, Weedies is your ideal sanctuary. Join us in celebrating the remarkable journey that is cannabis consumption and embark on a sensory adventure like no other.

Discover the art of cannabis at Weedies, where every visit promises relaxation, camaraderie, and a memorable experience you won't find anywhere else.
